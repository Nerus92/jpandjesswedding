Our Story

the artist & the engineer

Intro

"We Are All Stardust," reflects the wonder of our cosmic origins and the extraordinary chance that brought us together. Each of us is made from the same stardust—atoms that have journeyed through the universe for billions of years that were forged from ancient stars. This profound connection highlights the remarkable way our individual paths have aligned to create this special moment.

His connection

Looking back, it's awe-inspiring to consider the scientific marvel of how our lives intersected. Out of the infinite possibilities of how our atoms could have come together, the universe orchestrated our meeting in a way that feels both miraculous and deeply significant. The alignment of our stories is a beautiful testament to the randomness and beauty of cosmic chance.

Her connection

As we celebrate with our loved ones, we are reminded of how our unique journeys have intertwined. The presence of family and friends adds depth to our celebration, illustrating how each person's path has contributed to our shared story. This day is a tribute not only to our love but also to the meaningful connections that have shaped our lives.

Family link

The theme "We Are All Stardust" underscores the joy of sharing this milestone with those who have been part of our journey. It's a celebration of how the universe, with its boundless possibilities, has brought us together and gathered our loved ones for this occasion. The convergence of our stardust and the collective presence of our cherished guests highlight the wonder of this moment.

Excitement, close, sharing with you

As we embark on this new chapter, we are grateful for the cosmic forces that led us to each other. Our wedding is a celebration of the miraculous alignment of our lives and the excitement of starting our future surrounded by the people we hold dear. It's a reminder of the extraordinary nature of our connection and the joy of sharing it with all of you.
